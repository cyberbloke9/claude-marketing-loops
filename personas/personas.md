# TERREM Personas

Source: ANAROCK Homebuyer Sentiment Survey H1 2025 (~8,250 respondents, 14 cities — primary source, verified). Panel skews property-interested Indians, which matches TERREM's audience; do not generalize to all households. Refresh monthly via `/loop-persona`.

## P1 — The Upgrader (end-use, ~65% of market)

- **Who:** salaried professional, late 20s–40s, buying to live in. Budget ₹90L–1.5Cr (36% of market and growing; affordable <₹45L demand has collapsed to 17%).
- **Wants:** 3BHK in Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Delhi-NCR, Ahmedabad; 2BHK-leaning in MMR, Pune (Kolkata 46/46 tie).
- **Pain points (verified, in priority order):**
  1. **Price anxiety — 81%** worried about rising prices; only 21% will buy as planned; 71% face delays. Job-to-be-done: *"Tell me if I'm buying at the wrong time, before I commit."*
  2. **Completion risk — 98%** demand timely-completion assurance. JTBD: *"Tell me if this builder actually delivers."*
  3. **Construction quality — 93%.** Among dissatisfied affordable-segment buyers: 92% cite poor location, 90% poor construction/design. JTBD: *"Tell me what I can't see in the sample flat."*
- **Channels:** Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts. Owned: WhatsApp/email digest.
- **Content mode:** protective, specific, checkable. "Here's how to verify X in 2 minutes on TERREM."

## P2 — The Investor (~35% of market)

- **Who:** owns or has owned property; treats RE as an asset class. RE is India's #1 preferred asset (63%, vs stocks 22%, gold 7% — and rising +4% YoY).
- **Pain points / JTBD:**
  1. *"Which locality compounds — yield vs appreciation, with data?"*
  2. *"When can I exit?"* — liquidity by micro-market.
  3. *"What's the regulatory/RERA risk on this project or builder?"*
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, X, email digest. NRI sub-segment over-indexes on LinkedIn/email.
- **Content mode:** analytical, comparative, benchmark-driven. City Leaderboards live here.

# TERREM — Business Context for the Creative Engine

What every post ultimately serves. The creative engine reads this before choosing hook × format.

## What TERREM is

Indian real-estate **intelligence** platform — intel.terrem.in. Not a listings site, not a brokerage: it sells clarity on the largest financial decision of a person's life. Hyderabad-first (locality metrics, price indices, builder tracking, market simulations), expanding city-by-city.

## Business model

- **Free tier:** market intelligence content + basic locality/market views → the audience-building surface.
- **Sign-up gate:** combined sign-in wall + onboarding (live since v0.4.0, incl. email/password + OTP 2FA).
- **Premium tier:** deeper intelligence (full locality reports, builder risk, investment scores). <!-- pricing/packaging: FILL IN — not yet documented here -->
- **The flywheel (verified strategy, RESEARCH.md R10):** content → traffic → sign-ups → usage data & partnerships → richer intelligence → better content. Raw data is not the moat; synthesis + audience is.

## What a post must therefore DO (in priority order)

1. **Earn trust** — every number sourced and dated on-graphic; never a stat we can't defend. One integrity failure costs more than 100 posts gain.
2. **Drive a checkable action on TERREM** — each post ends in something the viewer can verify/do on intel.terrem.in in ~2 minutes. Impressions without clicks feed nothing.
3. **Build the habit** — recurring named formats (Weekly Ledger, leaderboards) so returning readers (WRR, the north star) compound. Retention beats reach ~5x (verified).
4. **Grow reach** — last, and only via 1–3.

## Constraints the engine must respect

- **Data provenance gate:** TERREM's own DB price-trends are BLOCKED for content until Path A lands (PLAN.md §2). Public verifiable data only, until then.
- **Never:** hype, fake urgency, unverified market predictions, blacklisted stats (RESEARCH.md), anything a skeptical CA aunt would flag.
- Audience personas + pain codes: `personas/personas.md` (P1 Upgrader 65% / P2 Investor 35%; PRICE 81% / DELIVERY 98% / QUALITY 93% / YIELD / EXIT / RERA).

# TERREM — Brand Context for the Creative Engine

Voice and visual identity above the token level. Tokens/type/chart mechanics live in `brand/brand-kit.md` (source of truth for the renderer); this file is the judgment layer.

## Voice: The Candid Analyst

Data-backed, mildly contrarian, protective of the buyer. "Says what builders won't." Dry wit, one beat max. Full do/don't table: brand-kit.md §1. Register check: the skeptical CA aunt AND the 29-year-old Bengaluru PM must both trust it.

## Visual identity

- **The chart/number IS the brand.** TERREM's "listing photo" is its data visual (RESEARCH.md B1). A TERREM post is recognizable by: cream canvas (#faf8f3), one teal accent (#0f766e) on the one element that matters, Inter, a source+date stamp, the wordmark. Nothing decorative.
- **Recognizable recurring formats** (PIPELINE-V2 §4): BIG NUMBER · TIMELINE · RECEIPTS · VS · LEADERBOARD · CHART · CHECKLIST. Format consistency is how card #47 gets recognized in-feed the way a Chartr post is.
- **One dominant element per surface** — a number or visual structure that works at 360px thumbnail. If the thumbnail doesn't stop a scroll, the card failed regardless of typography.
- **So-what line on-card, always** — the viewer's utility must survive a caption-less share.

## TERREM never does

- More than one accent color per surface; photos/textures under type; decorative fonts; memes/unhinged chaos (mechanics of Duolingo transfer — consistency + speed + arcs — the register does not).
- Truncated axes without disclosure; unsourced charts; "up to X%" weasel stats.
- Urgency theater ("last chance!", countdowns) — TERREM's urgency is only ever real (a RERA order's 45-day deadline is; a sale isn't).

## Wordmark

"TERREM" set in Inter 700, --accent-deep #0d3d38, bottom-right, small. On dark panels: white on #0d3d38.
